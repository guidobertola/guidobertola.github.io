Personal Profile

About me

Hello! I'm Guido Bertola, an enthusiastic and dedicated environmental engineer with a passion for sustainability. With over five years of experience in the water and wastewater treatment industry, I've honed my technical and leadership skills while always seeking innovative solutions for a greener future. My journey has taken me from leading commercial and technical teams to collaborating on research projects, all driven by a deep commitment to environmental responsibility.

I'm currently pursuing my Professional Science Master's degree in Environmental Analysis at Rice University, specializing in Sustainability. This experience has allowed me to further develop my knowledge and understanding of sustainable practices, renewable energies, and decarbonization strategies. I'm eager to contribute my skills and expertise to projects that promote triple-impact development and create a positive impact on the environment and society.

My international background has enriched my perspective and fostered a collaborative spirit. I'm fluent in Spanish, English, French, German, and Italian, enabling me to connect with diverse teams and stakeholders. I thrive in environments that encourage innovation and teamwork, always striving to learn from others and share my own knowledge and experiences.

Throughout my career, I've demonstrated strong analytical and problem-solving abilities, coupled with a dedication to delivering high-quality results. I'm passionate about finding practical solutions to complex environmental challenges and believe that sustainability should be at the forefront of every decision. I'm a firm believer in continuous learning and am always seeking opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills.

I invite you to explore my website to learn more about my background, experiences, and aspirations. Feel free to reach out if you'd like to connect or discuss potential collaborations. Let's work together to build a more sustainable future!

Work Experience

Rice University

Activate Program Coordinator and Communications Specialist

February 2024 - Present

As the Coordinator and Communication Specialist for the Activate Program, I focus on enhancing the design and technical communication skills of engineering students. A key part of my role involves analyzing tools like the "Grammarly" application to improve academic writing quality. This integration fosters a deeper understanding of effective communication practices among our students.

I also research innovative initiatives to continuously enhance our program offerings, ensuring that our curriculum meets the evolving needs of engineering students. By staying attuned to trends in technical communication, we aim to provide relevant support.

Data analysis is crucial for assessing the program's impact. By examining data from undergraduate and graduate courses, I can demonstrate our positive influence on students' communication capabilities and identify areas for improvement.

To raise awareness of our services, I generate engaging multimedia content that informs students about the resources available to them. By effectively communicating the benefits of the Activate Program, we empower students to succeed in their academic journeys.

Hewlett Packard Enterprise

Sustainability Technologies Intern

June 2024 - August 2024

During my internship at HPE HQ within the global Sustainable Innovation team, I focused on projects aimed at enhancing the environmental performance of the company’s products and services. One significant project involved researching different Internal Carbon Price models, such as implicit price, carbon fee, and shadow price, for potential corporate implementation. I compiled my findings into a detailed report and presentation with actionable recommendations.

I also created a Sustainability Maturity ranking, analyzing the environmental and technological performance of over 25 countries. This evaluation helped assess each market's potential to generate sustainability-enabled sales. Additionally, I defined metrics to measure the success of corporate sustainability initiatives, including Sustainable Design programs for software, hardware, and applications. To promote sustainable practices, I developed proposals highlighting the eco-friendly attributes of our products, including internal eco-labeling and a sustainable scoring system.

Bioingepro SRL

Technical & Commercial Leader

March 2016 - June 2023

In my role, I was responsible for searching and following up with potential clients, which included plant visits and meetings with technical staff and management. I analyzed incoming requests for quotations, gathering essential design information and prioritizing requests based on their relevance.

I designed custom solutions for the construction, operation, and revamping of water and wastewater treatment plants, encompassing various technologies such as chemical treatments, DAF systems, aeration systems, MBR, SBR, activated sludge, and disinfection methods. Additionally, I coordinated the budgeting department by defining team priorities and tasks, as well as revising and approving budgets for clients.

My responsibilities extended to generating and reviewing technical and commercial offers, including documents for both private and public tenders. I managed follow-ups with over 300 potential B2B clients and tracked project completions across a wide range of scales. I also generated quarterly commercial indicators, contributed to marketing efforts by writing biweekly technical articles and monthly newsletters, and participated in internal and external quality audits per ISO 9001:2015 standards. My role involved collaboration and knowledge exchange with both national and international partners to enhance our services.

As a Sales Representative at Bioingepro, I was responsible for verifying budgets and developing comprehensive commercial offers, ensuring that our proposals aligned with client needs and project specifications. My efforts were aimed at expanding our client base and enhancing our service offerings in the water treatment sector.

Centro Educativo Tecnológico ATILRA

Teaching Tutor

March 2016 - November 2017

Tutor (teaching assistant) on "Industrial Safety" of the "Technical Degree on Manufacture and Production of Food", at the Technological Studies Center ATILRA of Sunchales, Province of Santa Fe, Argentina.

ETH Zürich

Intern

August 2016 - November 2016

Internship at ETH Zürich, helping PhD students in the Grassland group of the Environmental Science Department.
